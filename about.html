About Me

Hello! I'm Orhun Ege Eker, a multidisciplinary designer passionate about combining art and technology to create engaging visual experiences. My work spans interactive installations, 3D modeling, animation, AI-generated visuals, and more. I enjoy pushing creative boundaries and exploring new tools in every project.

Software I use: Blender, Maverick Render, Adobe Creative Suite, TouchDesigner, Rhino, Unreal Engine, ZBrush, Houdini Side FX.
